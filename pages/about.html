Make it until it is perfect

Macal is an international company focused on architectural and interior design products. Our company values both our and clients' time, so we make our products unique and fully customizable for customers' needs. Our 30+ years of experience taught us that the best way to make things is by hands, so absolutely all of our exterior and interior parts are hand-made to ensure its top-notch quality and durability.

The company was founded in 1986 by George W. Macal in Madrid, Spain. Throughout the years of development and evolution, the main company's office moved to the New York City and is located there to this day. Our exact address is 285 Fulton Street, New York City, NY, USA.

We provide some of the best architectural and design services in the entire North America. Here are some of the things you can expect us to do:

  • Exterior Design Services
  • Interior Design Services
